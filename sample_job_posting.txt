Senior Backend Engineer - Platform Team

About the Role:
We are seeking a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team. You will design and build scalable microservices that power our e-commerce platform, serving millions of users worldwide.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and develop RESTful APIs and microservices using Python and Go
- Build and maintain distributed systems with high availability requirements
- Collaborate with frontend engineers and product managers to deliver features
- Optimize database queries and improve system performance
- Mentor junior engineers and lead technical initiatives
- Participate in on-call rotation for production support

Required Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in backend software development
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one other language (Go, Java, or Node.js)
- Deep understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS or GCP) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Solid understanding of system design, data structures, and algorithms
- Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills
- Strong communication and collaboration abilities

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with message queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Familiarity with monitoring tools like Datadog or Prometheus
- Experience in high-traffic e-commerce or fintech environments
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Remote-friendly work environment
- Health and wellness benefits
- Professional development budget
- Collaborative and inclusive team culture
